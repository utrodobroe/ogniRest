Предисловие

Ресторан Огни - место где все ваши гастрономические месты исполнятся.

Dinner in the canopy

The restaurant is situated on the 8th floor in Fælledparken (Common Gardens) in the center of Copenhagen,
from where one can follow the seasons' journey through the tree crowns,
catch glimpses of the city's green copper roofs and just see the windmills of Oeresund.
The location emphasizes Geranium's vision of gastronomic clarity and diversity.
We explore the area of tension between the urbane and the natural.

МЕНЮ

Roskoshnyy inter'yer restorana v Sankt-Peterburge byl sproyektirovan talantlivym dizaynerom Anastasiyey Khalchitskoy. Etot sovershenno novyy gastrobar s original'nym nazvaniyem «Vinovniki» otkrylsya po initsiative vladel'tsev izvestnoy seti Reca i distrib'yutorov vinnoy kompanii Simple. Dannoye zavedeniye predlagayet menyu a-la carte, predvaritel'no zakazannyye vinnyye obedy i vecherinki «dlya intellektualov». Osoboy gordost'yu Vinovnikov yavlyayetsya vinnaya karta, sostavlennaya odnim iz zvozdnykh somel'ye, zanimayushchikh 8-ye mesto v mire v spiske samykh izvestnykh znatokov etogo napitka. Sistema osveshcheniya dlinnogo zala sostoit iz neskol'kikh vidov svetil'nikov. The luxurious interior of the restaurant in St. Petersburg was designed by the talented designer Anastasia Khalchitskaya. This brand new gastrobar with the original name “Culprits” was opened on the initiative of the owners of the well-known Reca network and distributors of the Simple wine company. This establishment offers an a-la carte menu, pre-ordered wine dinners and parties “for intellectuals”. The culprits are especially proud of the wine list, compiled by one of the star sommeliers, occupying the 8th place in the world in the list of the most famous connoisseurs of this drink. The lighting system of a long hall consists of several types of lamps.
